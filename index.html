Your First Month Timeline

What to expect week by week during your first month at Amrita

Week 1: Orientation & Settling In

Attend orientation sessions, get your ID card, explore the campus, meet your hostel mates, and attend the welcome ceremony. Don't miss the Chakravyuha intro session!

Week 2: Classes Begin

Your regular classes start. Get familiar with your timetable, locate all your classrooms, and understand the lab schedules. Join at least one club's intro meeting.

Week 3: First Assessments

Small quizzes and assignments begin. Start building good study habits. Attend your first club workshop or event. Make friends in your branch!

Week 4: Finding Your Rhythm

By now, you should have a routine. Balance studies with extracurriculars. Participate in your first college event. Set your CGPA goals for the semester.
