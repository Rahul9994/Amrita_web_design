Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from real freshers. If you don't find your answer here, ask a senior!

What should I bring on my first day?

Bring your admission letter, ID proof, passport-size photos (at least 10), original certificates, a laptop, basic toiletries, bedsheets, and comfortable clothes. Don't overpack — you can buy most things in Amaravati. Keep digital copies of all documents on your phone and cloud storage.

Is the hostel compulsory for all students?

Hostel is compulsory for first-year students. From second year onwards, you can opt for day scholar status if you live nearby with family. However, most students prefer staying in the hostel even in later years because of the convenience and campus life experience.

Can I use my own laptop or do I need to buy a specific one?

You can use your own laptop. For CSE students, we recommend at least 8GB RAM, SSD storage, and a decent processor (i5/Ryzen 5 or above). A dedicated GPU is helpful for AI/ML work but not mandatory. The college has computer labs for students who don't have laptops.

What is the dress code?

Amrita follows a modest dress code. Formal/casual wear is acceptable for classes. Avoid shorts, sleeveless tops, and overly casual attire in academic areas. During lab sessions, wear closed shoes and avoid loose clothing. Traditional wear is always welcome and appreciated!

Can I go home on weekends?

Yes, you can go home on weekends with proper permission from the warden. You need to sign the outing register and return by the specified time (usually Sunday evening). During exam periods, weekend outings may be restricted. Always inform your parents and roommates when you leave.

How strict are the hostel rules?

Hostel rules are reasonable but strictly enforced. Key rules: No visitors of opposite gender in rooms, no alcohol/smoking, no loud music after 11 PM, mandatory attendance at night, and no cooking in rooms. Breaking rules can lead to fines or suspension. Respect the rules — they're for everyone's safety and comfort.

Is there WiFi in the hostel?

Yes! High-speed WiFi is available in all hostel blocks and academic areas. The password is provided on your ID card or during orientation. Speed is sufficient for streaming, video calls, and online learning. However, during peak hours (evening), the speed may reduce slightly due to heavy usage.

Can I change my branch after joining?

Branch change is possible after the first year based on your CGPA and availability of seats. You need to be among the top performers in your current branch. The process happens at the beginning of the second year. Focus on getting a high CGPA in Year 1 if you want to switch branches.

What about placements? When do they start?

Placement drives typically start from the 7th semester (pre-final year). However, internship opportunities begin from the 5th-6th semester. Companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Amazon, Microsoft, and many startups visit campus. Maintain a CGPA above 7.5 and build a strong skill set to maximize your chances.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes! Amrita offers merit-based scholarships for top performers. There are also scholarships for economically backward students, sports achievers, and cultural talents. Check with the admissions office or scholarship cell for detailed criteria and application procedures. Apply early!

How do I manage if I get sick?

The campus has a health center with a qualified doctor and nurse. For minor issues, visit the health center. For emergencies, the college provides ambulance service to nearby hospitals. Inform your warden and parents immediately. Keep a basic first-aid kit in your room.

Can I start my own club?

Absolutely! If you have a unique idea and can gather at least 15 interested students, you can propose a new club. Submit a proposal to the Student Affairs office with your club's vision, activities plan, and faculty advisor details. Approval usually takes 2-4 weeks.
